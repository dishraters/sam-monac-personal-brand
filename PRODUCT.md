# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small business owners, collaborators, SEO partners, and prospective clients who need to understand what Sam Monac does before they trust AI Business Boomer. They are arriving from search, referrals, email, or agent-generated handoffs and need a fast read on Sam's operator credibility.

## Product Purpose

This site is Sam's personal authority layer. It should make Sam visible as the person behind AI Business Boomer, explain his point of view on practical AI systems, and route commercial intent toward AIBB calls, services, and proof.

## Brand Personality

Operator-grade, candid, field-tested.

The page should feel like a working command desk for a hands-on business builder, not a polished founder vanity page.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI founder landing pages with beige backgrounds, vague metrics, gradient type, and identical cards.
- Personal portfolio pages that talk about vision without showing the operating system.
- Dark SaaS pages that feel like crypto dashboards or developer tooling.
- Fake proof: no invented clients, testimonials, rankings, awards, press, or revenue claims.

## Design Principles

1. Put Sam first: his name, point of view, and practical role must be obvious in the first viewport.
2. Route attention to AI Business Boomer: every commercial path should lead back to the main business.
3. Show the operating system: explain workflows, proof loops, and Hermes expansion as a system, not as scattered bullets.
4. Use real proof only: present current capabilities and proof themes without pretending completed case studies exist.
5. Make the page memorable enough to justify being separate from AIBB.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Keep contrast strong, preserve keyboard focus states, respect reduced motion, and avoid text sizes below 16px for meaningful content.
